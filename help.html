Medical Tests

Test Name Description Procedure Results
Blood Test A test that analyzes blood samples to assess overall health and detect various medical conditions. 1. A blood sample is collected from a vein.
2. The sample is sent to a laboratory for analysis.
3. Results are generated and shared with the healthcare provider.		 Results may include information about blood cell counts, cholesterol levels, glucose levels, and more.
Kidney Test A test that evaluates kidney function and detects any abnormalities or diseases affecting the kidneys. 1. A urine sample and blood sample are collected.
2. The samples are analyzed for markers of kidney function.
3. Results are generated and shared with the healthcare provider.		 Results may include information about kidney function, creatinine levels, electrolyte levels, and more.
Liver Test A test that assesses liver function and detects liver diseases or disorders. 1. A blood sample is collected.
2. The sample is analyzed for liver enzymes and other markers.
3. Results are generated and shared with the healthcare provider.		 Results may include information about liver enzyme levels, bilirubin levels, and more.
Lung Test A test that evaluates lung function and detects respiratory conditions or abnormalities. 1. The patient may be required to blow into a spirometer to measure lung capacity.
2. Other tests such as chest X-rays or CT scans may be performed to assess lung health.
3. Results are analyzed by a healthcare provider.		 Results may include information about lung capacity, airflow, and presence of any lung diseases or conditions.
